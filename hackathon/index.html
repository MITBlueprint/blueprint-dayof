Keynote Speakers

We are pleased to welcome to Blueprint 2015 two wonderful speakers who are heavily involved in the Computer Science Community:

Jeremy Rossmann

Jeremy Rossmann is a former MIT student and the cofounder of Make School, an online platform that aims to teach students of any age how to build and ship products. Jeremy started Make School while he was a student in 2012. Now Make School has taught over 500 students through in-person courses and over a million students through online tutorials. Make School started off teaching students how to build iPhone games, and now they are here to teach you how to build your first iOS game!

Baris Yüksel

Baris Yüksel is an MIT alum who graduated with a BS in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science and is now a senior software engineer at Google. He currently works on bridging the gap between Google Search and Google Now, an app that tells you exactly what you need right now by pulling data from other Google services. Outside of work, he enjoys teaching computer science to the developing world and has a passion for spreading this knowledge to transform the human experience for a better tomorrow. He loves to talk about machine learning, so feel free to ask him a question about it!
