General Information

Questions?

If you have questions, ask us - we're friendly! Find a staff member, call 224-900-BLUE to chat with a Blueprint Organizer, or send us an email at blueprint@hackmit.org! We're happy to help. In the case of an emergency, call the MIT Police at 617-253-1212.

Security Policy

Blueprint would not be possible without MIT’s support, so we ask that you respect all university property and stay within designated areas.

For the learnathon, this means that you must stay on Stata’s first floor, and for the hackathon, this means that you must stay on the Media Lab’s sixth floor. You can’t leave the building without checking out at the help desk.

Once you leave this area, you won’t be allowed back in unless your parent has emailed us before the event granting you permission to wander off. Your mentors will be taking attendance throughout the day, so we will be informed of your absence.

Laptop Rental

If you’d like to rent a laptop, come to the Help Desk and check one out. You’ll need to return this by the end of the day on both days.
