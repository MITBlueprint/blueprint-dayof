Judging & Prizes

Project submissions must be in by 5:30 PM, but you can continue hacking until the expo at 6 PM. At the expo, each team will present their projects for a few minutes at a time to small groups of judges in a science-fair format. After expo judging ends at 7 PM, the top teams will be chosen to demo onstage during our closing ceremony in front of all of Blueprint!

The rookie division is meant for people learning to build their first projects or attending their first hackathon this Blueprint. Otherwise, join the experienced division!
