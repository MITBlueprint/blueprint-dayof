Logistics Info/FAQs
Got a question not in this list? Go to the Helpdesk! Or if you’re not in Johnson, call our hotline at 507-307-HACK.
How do I connect to wifi?
In Johnson arena, connect to the
hackmit14
network with password
fancycat
. Everywhere else at MIT, connect to open network MIT GUEST.
I’m tired! How do I get an air mattress, meet my overnight host and go to sleep?
Call your host, ask them to pick you up, and get an air mattress from Helpdesk in Johnson!
Last year, there was a “lockdown” in Johnson overnight. Is there a lockdown this year?
Nope, there is no lockdown this year. As long as you have your badge on, you will be able to come and go from Johnson as you please.
I need technical help. How do I get help on [API, language]?
We have a help queue and mentors to help you along!
I’m hungry! How do I get food?
We’ll have food events throughout the day/night, check the day-of booklet or this website for information. We also have an abundant supply of healthy and non-healthy snacks at the concession stand in Johnson hallway.
Something isn’t working! I’m confused! Where’s nyan cat???
Please come to the Helpdesk and tell us--we’ll be happy to help!
When do I need to submit my project by?
Upload your project to ChallengePost (link soon) by 9AM on Sunday 10/5. Judging will begin at 10:30AM, so you may update your project until that time.
When do I need to form my team by?
We will have a team formation event from 12:30-1:15pm on Saturday. You must submit your team and location (table number) by 2pm.
How does judging work?
We will have a judging expo on Sunday from 10:30-11:45am. Feel free to wander around and check out other projects, but make sure at least one of your team members is at your table to present to judges.
